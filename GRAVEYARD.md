toward 30, the strongest revivals are flagged ⭐.

- **The Context Hoarder** — Pastes the entire codebase into the window every time. Treats the token limit as a personal insult. _(AI cluster overfull)_
- **The Reanimator** — Bends elements to their will at 60fps, speaks in springs and shared values, makes things slide that need not slide. _(merged into The Gesture Handler)_

- **The First-Question Gladiator** — Reaches the mic before the applause fades. "More of a comment than a question." _(visually collides with Merge Conflict Gladiator)_
- **The Swag Forager** ⭐ — Optimized the sponsor-booth loop for maximum t-shirt yield. Tote bag full by 10am. _(first to revive when restocking conference cards)_
- **The Sticker Diplomat** — Laptop lid is a treaty map of every conference attended. Trades stickers like currency. _(character is a laptop lid; weak as a figure to draw)_

- **The Flag Gardener** — Tends a sprawling garden of feature flags, none removed, can't remember which are on in prod. Something in there has gone feral. _(process cluster already deep)_
